## 3. Design Direction

- **Vibe**: minimalist, modern, boutique/editorial — closer to a high-end fashion lookbook than a typical grocery store site. Confident use of negative space. Photography is the hero of every section; text plays a supporting role.
- **Color palette**:
  - Dark green (primary background/structure) — around `#14261C` or `#0F2E1D`
  - Black (text, structural elements) — `#0A0A0A`
  - Gold (accents only — dividers, icons, hover states, key CTAs, small detail lines) — `#C9A24B` or `#D4AF37`
  - Use gold sparingly as an accent, never as a dominant fill. Ensure gold text on dark green/black passes accessible contrast standards.
- **Typography**: pair a refined serif or high-contrast display serif for headings (evokes heritage and elegance — e.g. Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond, or similar) with a clean modern sans-serif for body copy (e.g. Inter, Work Sans, or similar).
- Subtle traditional motifs (thin gold geometric line patterns inspired by habesha kemis embroidery) can be used sparingly as section dividers — never overwhelming the modern aesthetic.
- Fully image-forward: large, full-bleed photography, generous whitespace, minimal copy per section.
